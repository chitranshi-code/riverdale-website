About Riverdale


Riverdale is a dark teen drama based on Archie Comics, set in a small town hiding dangerous secrets beneath its calm surface.


  • The story begins with the mysterious murder of Jason Blossom.
  • Archie Andrews struggles between music, love, and responsibility.
  • Betty Cooper appears perfect but battles inner darkness and family secrets.
  • Veronica Lodge tries to escape her criminal father’s shadow.
  • Jughead Jones investigates crimes and narrates the story.
  • Each season focuses on a new central mystery.
  • The show explores crime, trauma, corruption, and identity.
  • Relationships are intense, emotional, and sometimes toxic.
  • Later seasons introduce time jumps and alternate realities.
  • The final season blends nostalgia with emotional closure.
